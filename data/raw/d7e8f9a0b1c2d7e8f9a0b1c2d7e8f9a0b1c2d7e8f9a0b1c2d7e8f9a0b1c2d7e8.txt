RAG System Internal Notes
=========================
Author: project team
Date: 2026-07-08

These are rough working notes captured during the initial design session.
They are not polished — just enough to remember the decisions we made.

--- Embedding model choice ---
We evaluated three options:
  1. OpenAI text-embedding-3-small  (1536-dim, cheap, fast)
  2. OpenAI text-embedding-3-large  (3072-dim, better recall, 5x cost)
  3. Sentence-Transformers all-MiniLM-L6-v2  (384-dim, local, free)

Decision: start with text-embedding-3-small for the prototype. Switch to
large only if recall metrics fall below 0.85 on our eval set.

--- Chunk size ---
After some quick experiments on our internal docs corpus:
  - 256 tokens: too fragmented, retrieval returns noisy results
  - 512 tokens: good balance
  - 1024 tokens: accurate but burns context budget quickly

Going with 512 tokens, 64-token overlap.

--- Metadata we want to keep per chunk ---
  - source filename or URL
  - page number (PDFs) or section heading (HTML/Markdown)
  - date of the source document
  - content hash of the original file

--- Open questions ---
  - Do we need a re-ranker? Probably yes once the corpus grows past 10k docs.
  - Should we store raw HTML or strip to plain text before embedding?
    Leaning toward stripping — HTML tags add noise to embeddings.
  - Eval framework: RAGAS looks promising, need to test it.
